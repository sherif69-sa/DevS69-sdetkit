# Adoption walkthrough: small team (2-week rollout)

## Week 1

1. Run `make first-proof` locally and confirm SHIP/NO-SHIP output.
2. Add CI `make first-proof-verify` lane.
3. Review first-proof troubleshooting doc for top failure modes.

## Week 2

1. Enable weekly trend + threshold artifact review.
2. Route owner escalations from threshold breaches.
3. Capture one internal screencast of lane operation for onboarding.
