# 12-Month Execution Roadmap (Execution Step 5)

## Goal

Translate strategy into month-by-month execution with explicit owners, measurable outputs, and gate decisions.

---

## Annual north-star outcomes

- Predictable qualified-pipeline generation.
- 3-5 successful pilots with clear KPI deltas.
- 2+ paid conversions and repeatable expansion motion.
- Enterprise-ready governance narrative with trust assets.

---

## Quarter themes

| Quarter | Theme | Strategic question |
|---|---|---|
| Q1 | Foundation + pilots | Do we have message-market-process fit? |
| Q2 | Repeatability + expansion | Can we scale conversion predictably? |
| Q3 | Enterprise packaging | Can we win governance-led buyers? |
| Q4 | Efficiency + retention | Can we grow with durable unit economics? |

---

## Monthly execution plans

### Month 1: January

**Quarter context:** Q1 (foundation + first conversions).

**Primary focus:** Foundation.

**Core objective:** Finalize positioning, ICP scoring rollout, and KPI baseline instrumentation.

#### Outcome targets
- Pipeline: stage progression targets achieved.
- Pilot: quality and cycle-time targets achieved.
- Commercial: objection resolution and proposal quality improved.
- Operations: KPI dashboard completeness >= 95%.

#### Work packages
1. GTM execution package
2. Pilot success package
3. Commercial/package package
4. Measurement and governance package

#### Key deliverables
- Monthly operating review
- Updated dashboard with trend commentary
- Risk register and mitigation actions
- Next-month operating plan

#### Owners
- Founder/GM (decision rights + enterprise deals)
- GTM lead (pipeline + conversion)
- Solutions lead (pilot outcomes)
- Ops analyst (KPI data quality)

#### Gate criteria
- Gate 1: qualified pipeline target met
- Gate 2: active pilots on-plan or corrected
- Gate 3: KPI instrumentation complete and trustworthy
- Gate 4: clear next-month priority map approved

#### Risks and mitigations
- Risk: weak discovery quality
  - Mitigation: tighten qualification script and ICP guardrails
- Risk: pilot scope creep
  - Mitigation: enforce fixed-scope charter and weekly gate checks
- Risk: procurement delay
  - Mitigation: legal/commercial path starts by pilot midpoint

#### Month-close questions
1. What converted and why?
2. What stalled and why?
3. What should we stop next month?
4. What is the single highest-leverage change for next month?
